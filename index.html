Movement & Positioning

The heatmap shows a clear concentration inside the penalty area, particularly on the right side of the box. His physicality and work rate allow him to contribute significantly in defensive phases, making him effective in pressing systems.

At Hødd, he transitioned into a more structured attacking setup. Despite this shift, his performance remained stable. This indicates a profile that is transferable across systems and not dependent on a specific tactical structure.
